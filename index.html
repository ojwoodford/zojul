Come together

The Zojul network: meet then click

On Zojul, your interaction with other users is always in the real world. It's meet, then click; not click, then meet. There are no profiles to write, no groups to join, no photo albums, no messaging, commenting, forums, poking, winking, liking, hash-tagging… well, you get the picture. While those things can be great, we just want to complement them, encouraging real connections too. So, if after meeting, you want to become best Facebook buddies or tweet to each other until the cows come home, you can! But we rather hope you'll keep hanging out in the real world as well.

Alpha?

Zojul is new. Brand spanking new. To be honest, it's nowhere near finished. That's what the alpha means. So we're looking for a small group of adventurous souls who are up for trying something new and helping to make it better. Of course, while in alpha, the service will be completely free. Not only that, but if you attend three or more events during alpha, and fill out our feedback forms each time, we'll give you free membership for life. However there won't be a huge number of you, so we need you all to be in the same place. And that place is Cambridge, UK, because that's where we are, and we'd like to meet and talk to you too! If you're not in or near Cambridge, but like the sound of our service, do sign up anyway and we'll invite you to use the beta (the more polished, but not quite finished service).
