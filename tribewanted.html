Life is better shared with friends

Zojul's mission is to make it really easy to meet, connect and forge great friendships (and relationships) with your kind of people. To do this, we need input from a group of people (we call them our "test tribe") who believe in that mission, and are willing to:

  • Talk with us (in person, over coffee) about their experiences of meeting new people - pain points especially.
  • Use the first version of our platform (free, of course!) to meet other people at small social events.
  • Provide us with frank feedback on their experiences at the events and of our platform, and what new features they'd like to see.

If you're up for helping us create something wonderful, please leave your email below, and we'll be in touch about arranging that coffee. Please note that you are not committing to anything here; you can withdraw from our test tribe at any time.
